Geography & GIS


Welcome to my portfolio site!

My name is Sage, and I'm a geographer, artist, and a recent graduate from DePaul University where I earned my degree in Geography, with a concentration in GIS & Geotechnology and a minor in Graphic Design. As a geographer, my areas of interest include mapping land use and green space accessibility in Chicago, the effects of globalization and industry in Gujarat, India, and analysis of landscape based in theories of Cultural Geography.
This portfolio website is a combination of a personal project in learning and practicing html while also giving myself a space to display the various projects I've worked such as research papers, GIS projects, art, and graphic design work. Feel free to explore my website to learn more about how I've been applying my skills, experiences, and the ways my interests overlap.
